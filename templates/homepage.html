Heart Disease Prediction



Welcome to our Heart Disease Prediction Website, an AI-powered tool designed to help users assess their risk of heart disease using key health indicators such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and more. This application is built using machine learning techniques trained on the widely-used UCI Heart Disease Dataset, and aims to support early detection and awareness of cardiovascular risk. While the tool provides quick predictions based on user inputs, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice or diagnosis. Our goal is to demonstrate how technology and healthcare can work together to empower users to make informed decisions about their well-being.
